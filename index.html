    Calorie deficit

    Aim for 500 to 750 kcal below maintenance per day. This gives 0.5 to 1 kg loss per week without losing muscle. If weight stops dropping for 2 weeks, reduce rice portion slightly.

    Measure portions

    1 cup cooked rice, 150 g chicken or fish, 2 tsp oil total per day. Even clean food causes fat gain if you overeat. Use a kitchen scale or measuring cup.

    Protein every meal

    Eggs, fish, chicken, dal, soya chunks, and boiled motor dal. Hit 120 to 150 g protein daily to preserve muscle while losing fat. This is the single most important diet rule.

    Rules and guide

    What actually makes progress happen.

    Follow these rules consistently. Missing one session is fine. Missing three in a row breaks the habit.

    Progressive overload

    When 3 sets of 12 reps feel easy, add 2 to 3 kg next session. This is the most important training rule. Without it, your body adapts and fat loss slows after 3 to 4 weeks.

    Sleep 7 to 8 hours

    Poor sleep raises hunger hormones directly. Studies show adding 1 to 2 hours of sleep reduces calorie intake by around 270 kcal per day. Sleep is not optional for fat loss.

    Weekly weigh-in

    Weigh yourself every Monday morning after waking up. Daily weight fluctuates from water and food. The Monday number is the one that counts. If it is not dropping after 2 weeks, reduce rice at dinner.

    Drink 3.5 to 4 L water

    Chia seeds and oats absorb a lot of water. Dehydration mimics hunger and causes bloating. Drink plain water throughout the day, not just during workouts.

    Oil limit: 2 tsp per day

    Oil is pure fat and the easiest way to accidentally overshoot your calorie limit. Buy a small measuring spoon. Use a maximum of 2 tsp total across all your meals for the day.

    Why 5 exercises per day

    Each exercise has 3 sets, giving 15+ working sets per session. For a beginner, more volume than this causes soreness that stops you showing up the next day. Quality over quantity builds the habit first.
